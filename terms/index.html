Terms

Greenwashing

Is the way companies market eco-friendly products that in practice affect the environment the same or worse.

30% of our respondents believed it meant washing in an eco-friendly way

All Natural

Is not regulated by the FDA. Anything can claim to be 'All Natural'

Only 20% of respondents knew there are no enforced definitions.

Natural Products

Are not necessarily better for the environment. Check the labels before buying.

60% of respondents believed all-natural products were better for the environment
